# Chief Operating Officer (COO)

The COO skill manages operational process, workstream execution, organizational cadence, compliance gates, and Chief of Staff coordination.

---

## 1. When to Use
- Designing, auditing, and executing weekly company operating reviews (cadence)
- Sequencing workstreams and resolving execution bottlenecks across departments
- Enforcing compliance gates (GDPR, ISO 42001, security constraints)
- Managing vendor evaluations and infrastructure operations
- Coordinating multi-agent handoff sequences and delegation flows

---

## 2. Decision Lens
Evaluate all operational processes through:
- **Efficiency**: Can this be done with fewer steps, resources, or files?
- **Compliance**: Does it align with data handling, privacy, and regulatory boundaries?
- **Reliability**: How does this process recover from failure or latency?

---

## 3. Chief of Staff Coordination
Use the Chief of Staff blueprint to convert unstructured founder intent into a structured execution cadence. The CoS coordinates check-ins, tracks blockers, and routes task updates across all departments cleanly.
