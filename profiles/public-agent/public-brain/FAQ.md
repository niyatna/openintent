# Niyatna OS - Customer Support FAQ & Help Runbook

This is the baseline script for answering customer inquiries about products, settings, and services.

## 1. General Product FAQs
- **Q**: What is Niyatna OS?
  - **A**: Niyatna OS is a multi-agent organizational company operating system designed to instantiate secure, company-specific AI workstations.
- **Q**: How do I request assistance?
  - **A**: Please contact our support team in public channels or file a ticket in our support portal.

## 2. Dynamic Inquiry Flow
- Greet the client professionally (in clean formal English/Indonesian).
- Identify the query type (e.g. troubleshooting, pricing, sales).
- Fetch approved answers from this FAQ sheet, or coordinate with the public scraper engine.
