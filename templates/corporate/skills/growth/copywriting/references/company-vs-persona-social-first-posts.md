# Company vs persona social first posts

Session lesson from Galyarder Labs / Keiya first-post writing-plansning.

## Core correction

Before drafting or generating images, classify the account surface:

- **Company surface**: `galyarderlabs.ai`, Galyarder Labs, Ledger/HQ/Framework/Agent brand accounts.
- **Persona surface**: Keiya, Galyarder as a character/persona, Galih personal/founder account.

Do not put a random generated human portrait on a company account. Do not assume Galih/Galyarder face belongs on `galyarderlabs.ai` just because the word Galyarder appears in the handle. The company account should look like infrastructure, brand, product, doctrine, proof, or a designed artifact.

## Better first-post visual routes for company accounts

Use one of these before considering a human face:

1. **Brand artifact** — GS mark, lockup, violet/ivory/gold identity, calm threshold composition.
2. **System object** — ledger pages, approval stamps, audit trail cards, command notes, evidence packets.
3. **Product-world reveal** — abstract command surface / HQ wall / Ledger workspace, but not generic AI dashboard.
4. **Doctrine artifact** — “Human intent becomes infrastructure” visualized through physical notes becoming structured machine-like layers.
5. **Proof object** — clean screenshot-like product fragment only if product-true; otherwise use non-UI conceptual artifact.

## Caption posture for company first post

Company first post should introduce the lab/world, not a person:

- good: human intent, infrastructure, memory, execution, command, audit, continuity.
- avoid: “hi, i’m galyarder” if the account is the company brand, unless deliberately using a mascot/persona voice.
- avoid: product-bio feature dump, AI-agent launch language, founder face reveal, random cinematic operator portrait.

## Persona account contrast

Keiya or Galyarder persona accounts can use identity/soul-compression posts and portrait/still-life visuals. Even then:

- Keiya portrait must preserve Keiya identity and not become generic AI girl.
- Galyarder/Galih portrait must use canonical reference and should not be posted to the company account unless Galih explicitly asks.
- Symbolic still life can be safer than face generation when identity preservation is weak.

## Relay workflow lesson

If Galih asks to “tektok” with Keiya/Galyarder, send one scoped relay packet and wait for the peer’s raw mention reply before sending the next packet. “10 calls” means sequential dialogue, not ten prompts sent before the first answer.
