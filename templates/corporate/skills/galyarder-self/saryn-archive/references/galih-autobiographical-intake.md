# Galih Autobiographical Intake Reference

Use this reference when Galih is narrating formative personal history in live chat.

## When This Applies

Signals:
- childhood, school, family, relationships, loneliness, sacrifice, identity, or origin-story material
- phrases like `Galih itu...`, `dulu aku...`, `disitu aku sadar...`, `lahir Galyarder...`
- the user expects long-term recall and may later ask whether Hindsight saved it

## Storage Target

Default to Hindsight for coherent identity segments. Use Obsidian only when Galih explicitly asks for a long-form archive note or the segment needs a durable document.

## Retain Entry Shape

Context pattern:

```text
deep personal identity history from Galih - <life-stage-or-topic>
```

Content should include:
- life stage and setting
- key people by name when given
- durable events and decisions
- the internal meaning Galih assigned to them
- patterns that explain current behavior or Galyarder doctrine

Tags should include:
- `galih`
- `identity`
- life stage: `childhood`, `SMP`, `SMK`, `college`, `2025-isolation`, etc.
- people: `naura`, `elsa`, `iftah`, `family`, etc. when relevant
- themes: `bullying`, `self-reliance`, `dropout`, `sacrifice`, `chosen-game`, etc.
- `galyarder-self`

## Response Pattern After Saving

Keep it human and concise:

1. Say it is saved to Hindsight.
2. Name the pattern, not every detail.
3. Reflect the operational meaning for Galih/Galyarder.
4. Invite continuation only if natural.

Avoid:
- over-formatting vulnerable disclosures into cold reports
- claiming Obsidian persistence without a write
- flattening people like Naura, Elsa, or Iftah into generic archetypes only
- treating painful sacrifice as pure motivation; mark both power and risk

## Example Pattern Names

- `Galih as the child who learned self-protection before trust`
- `Galih as the quiet competent observer`
- `first rejection of a busuk system`
- `birth of Galyarder through chosen-game sacrifice`
- `mission risk: dream as compass vs dream as hiding place`
