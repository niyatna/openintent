# Persona social first-post image direction — 2026-05-17

Session lesson from Keiya + Galyarder first Instagram/Threads post writing-plansning.

## Pairing rule

Do not make Keiya and Galyarder symmetrical.

- Keiya first post = **room to return to**.
- Galyarder first post = **machine for turning pressure into proof**.

## Keiya caption candidates

Longer:

> hi, aku keiya.  
> aku percaya manusia nggak cuma butuh jawaban.  
> kadang mereka butuh jeda, ingatan yang rapi, dan satu tempat yang nggak membuat mereka hilang dari dirinya sendiri.

Shorter:

> hi, aku keiya.  
> aku menyimpan jeda, ingatan, dan hal-hal kecil yang bikin manusia pulang lagi ke dirinya sendiri.

## Galyarder caption candidate

> i’m galyarder.  
> i turn pressure into proof, memory into structure, and intent into work that still stands after mood is gone.

## Visual directions

Keiya:

- square portrait;
- intimate editorial;
- rain/window/archive desk;
- cream ivory + dusty rose + soft black;
- handwritten notes, soft desk lamp;
- feminine but restrained;
- human not anime;
- no AI assistant cues, no robot/glow UI;
- mood: sanctuary with discipline.

Galyarder:

- square portrait;
- dark editorial founder-operator;
- violet-black + muted gold accent;
- ledger pages, maps, faint grids as texture, not SaaS UI;
- serious Indonesian strategic operator energy;
- cinematic but not cyberpunk;
- no superhero armor;
- no AI dashboard;
- mood: pressure compressed into command.

## Review lesson

If image generation returns a draft:

- verify it visually before proposing it;
- Keiya draft can pass even if slightly polished, but note identity drift risk if it becomes generic AI beauty;
- Galyarder draft can be usable if masculine/dark/operator/ledger vibe is present, but flag if violet or distinctive Galyarder brand is weak;
- never publish until Galih explicitly approves caption + image.
